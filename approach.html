OUR GENESIS & MISSION

A relationship-driven credit partner.

Capitar was established in 2022 as a premier Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) to bridge the funding gap for emerging Indian startups, serving as a tech-enabled credit alternative to traditional commercial banks. Underpinned by over a century of cumulative debt underwriting and structuring experience, the firm provides structured Venture Debt solutions tailored for scale.

We aspire to be a trusted, one-stop credit partner for India's emerging businesses, designing risk-mitigated debt solutions that enhance value for all stakeholders, deliver consistent yields to investors, and preserve founder control.

Our underwriting framework evaluates startups beyond pure accounting metrics. By analyzing customer cohorts, contract stability, cash runways, and cap tables, we design customized, founder-first credit facilities that support operational scaling.

INVESTMENT PRINCIPLES

The principles that guide our underwriting.

Our Genesis

First startup specialized to underwrite AIF/Venture Debt, filling the funding gap for Indian emerging businesses with flexible credit solutions as a bank alternative.

Our Mission

To act as a trusted, one-stop credit partner. We deliver risk-mitigated structures that preserve founder equity, optimize investor returns, and build long-term relationships.
