INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

Asymmetric risk-return profile.

Capitar Ventures Trust operates as a SEBI-registered Category II Alternative Investment Fund. Our strategy focuses on generating predictable, high-yield fixed income through structured loans, supplemented by equity warrants that capture company valuation expansions.

We mitigate loss through strict underwriting parameters, focusing exclusively on companies backed by premier venture capital funds, boasting stable recurring revenues, and preserving 6+ months of cash runway.

By investing in private credit rather than early equity, we secure structured liquidity and senior charge on assets, offering strong downside protection relative to pure venture capital vehicles.

RISK MANAGEMENT

Strict compliance and governance frameworks.

Capitar implements rigorous check-and-balance systems at every lifecycle phase of the fund.

Credit Committee

Facilities are reviewed by an independent investment and credit risk committee, requiring unanimous approval prior to commitment issuance.

Concentration Limits

We maintain strict portfolio diversification parameters, capping exposure to any single sector or individual startup group at 10% of total fund capital.

Macro Stress-testing

We continuously model macro interest rate shifts, valuation cooling periods, and funding slowdown cycles to test portfolio resiliency.
