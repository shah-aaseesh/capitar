WHY VENTURE DEBT

Strategic capital designed for founders who value their equity.

01

Non-dilutive Growth

Unlike equity which dilutes your ownership permanently, venture debt provides growth capital with nominal or zero dilution, letting you retain upside.

02

Runway Extension

Extend your financial runway by 6 to 12 months between rounds, ensuring you achieve key performance milestones before pricing your next equity raise.

03

Capital Efficiency

Lower the weighted average cost of capital. Debt is structured for specific operational catalysts, optimizing working capital dynamics.
